Sleeping Bags for the Homeless

"Sleeping Bags for the Homeless Bay Area" is an all-volunteer, nonprofit effort to collect and distribute sleeping bags, blankets, tents, tarps, water and other supplies to the homeless in Silicon Valley in California.
